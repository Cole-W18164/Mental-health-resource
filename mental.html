Mental Health

This is a resource for people who want to know how to help those who are close to them with Mental health as a whole.

But first we need to know what mental health really is. Mental health is the wellbeing of someones emotional, psycological and social well-being. These three parts are the fundamental building blocks of having a healthy mental state.
The two parts of mental health that will be focused on in this site will be anxiety and depression.
